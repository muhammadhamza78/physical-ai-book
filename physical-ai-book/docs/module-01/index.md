# Chapter 1: Foundations of Physical AI

This chapter introduces you to the fundamentals of Physical AI systems.

## Chapter Overview

In this chapter, you'll explore:

1. **Lesson 1: Understanding Physical AI** - Learn what Physical AI is and why it matters
2. **Lesson 2: Sensing the World** - Work with sensors to gather environmental data
3. **Lesson 3: Acting on Information** - Use actuators to respond to sensor input
4. **Chapter Project** - Build your first complete Physical AI system

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this chapter, you will be able to:

- Define Physical AI and explain its real-world applications
- Connect and read data from various sensors
- Control actuators based on sensor input
- Build a simple Physical AI system from scratch

Let's begin with [Lesson 1: Understanding Physical AI](./lesson-01-understanding-physical-ai.md).
